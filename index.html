How it works?



TronzBank uses the Tronz Protocol to shield your transfers. Tronz Protocol is the official-supported privacy protocol of Tron Network. For users, TronzBank is just like a regular wallet.   • What’s the pricing of TronzBank?
    The service charge is 0.3% of your transfer amount.

  • Is TronBank open-source and audited?
    Yes. TronzBank is open-source and audited. The repository is at URL and the audition report is at URL.

  • Why need to deposit more tokens to make a withdrawal?
    ‍Due to Tronz restrictions, there is a minimum amount required for transfers. See here for details. To avoid this, we recommend using a pre-set amount.

  • Why TronzBank recommends using pre-set amounts for deposits and withdrawals?
    The idiosyncratic amounts will make on-chain analysis easier, and an attacker may be able to associate transactions with you by idiosyncratic amounts. For maximum protection of your privacy, we recommend using pre-set amounts.
